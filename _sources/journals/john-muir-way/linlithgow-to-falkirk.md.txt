## Getting There

**Note:** We walked this section as part of the Union Canal path, therefore there are some detours. 

- We took the John Muir Cyclepath that continues along the canal instead of entering Hallglen. Note that there is a [narrow underpass under the railway line](https://storymaps.arcgis.com/stories/2a120b71105a44dca23d4e110544e2ac#ref-n-PGCMTx) should you want to follow the John Muir Way footpath.
- We wanted to experience the eerie Falkirk Tunnel and therefore choose the towpath to Falkirk High to join the John Muir Way. 

**Start:** Linlithgow Avon Aqueduct
- **Train**: Linlithgow Station
- **Bus**: Midland Bluebird services around town

**End:** Falkirk
- **Train**: Falkirk High Station
- **Bus**: Midland Bluebird services around town
